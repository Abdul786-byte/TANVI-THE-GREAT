About the Event

HackSphere is IIT Bombay's flagship student hackathon — 48 hours of building, learning, and connecting with the brightest minds across India. Whether you're a coder, designer, or product thinker, this is your arena. Bring your team, bring your ideas, and let's build something remarkable.

Top 3 teams win cash prizes, internship opportunities, and mentorship from leading startup founders.
